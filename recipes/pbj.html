Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

An American Classic

The humble peanut butter and jelly sandwich. Simple, yes, but tasty comfort food that I enjoy most days.

Ingredients:

  • Natural peanut butter (I like Adam's)
  • Real fruit jelly
  • 2 slices of bread of choice (I prefer multigrain)

Steps:

  1. Spread generous helping of peanut butter across one slice of bread
  2. Spead generous helping of jelly on other slice of bread
  3. Press two slices together so peanut butter and jelly touch
  4. Enjoy!
