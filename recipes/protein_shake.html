Protein Shake

A Healthy Meal

A Berry protein shake is a great to start the day, to recover from a hard workout, or just to enjoy to hit your daily protein intake. It is simple to make, tastes amazing, and is super healthy to boot!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup 2% milk (I go lactose free)
  • 1 scoop chocolate whey protein powder
  • 1/2 cup forzen mixed berries

Steps:

  1. Pour milk into blender
  2. Add mixed berries and protein powder
  3. Blend on highest setting until berries completely liquified
  4. Pour into glass and enjoy
