Protein Pancakes

A simple, high protein breakfast

This recipe comes courtesy of 4x Mr. Olympia Jay Cutler. He featured this on his website many years ago as a go to when he was travelling. I can attest that it is not only easy to make, but delicious to boot!

Ingredients:

  • Note: all of this can be scaled as long as you use equal parts of each item
  • 1/2 cup liquid egg whites
  • 1/2 cup fat free cottage cheese
  • 1/2 cup quick oats

Steps:

  1. Mix all ingredients in a blender on highest setting until a smooth batter is formed
  2. Pour batter into non-stick pan over medium heat until desired pancake size is reached
  3. Cook until golden-brown, flipping once to cook both sides
  4. Drizzle with butter or your favorite syrup and enjoy
